Rules

  1. You must indicate whether or not you wish to participate by Tuesday, December 24, 2013 at 6pm PT.
  2. You must submit the following items to Cameron by Thursday, December 27, 2013 at 6pm PT.

    • A photo of how it is "wrapped". Gift wrapping can be bigger on the inside. ;-)

    • +1's should provide email and shipping addresses when they RSVP
  6. Players will take turns in random order, with each player having one turn.
  7. During a player's turn he may choose an unopened gift or steal a gift from another player.
  8. If a player has a package stolen from them, they get a free turn.
  9. A gift can be stolen twice, so that the third person who owns it is the final owner.
  10. The first person who plays gets a free turn at the end of the game.
